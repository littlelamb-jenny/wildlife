Our History

Amuay Wildlife Park has a rich history dating back to its establishment in [year]. Originally conceived as a sanctuary to protect the diverse flora and fauna indigenous to the region, the park has evolved into a premier nature reserve recognized internationally for its conservation efforts.

Over the years, the park has witnessed significant milestones, including the expansion of protected areas, the implementation of sustainable management practices, and collaborations with local communities and international organizations.

Today, Amuay Wildlife Sanctuary stands as a testament to the commitment of all those involved in its preservation, serving as a vital habitat for countless species and a source of inspiration for visitors from around the world.

Rescue

Too many animals are exploited and abused in places such as laboratories, circuses, roadside zoos and the illegal pet trade.

Amuay Wildlife Sanctuary offers sanctuary to many animals that have been persecuted and hunted, providing them with a safe haven.

  • Brown Bears
  • Wolves
  • Lynx
  • Wild Boar

We offer them immediate medical care, an environment tailored to their needs, food, and enrichment to promote their well-being.

Our Mission

At Amuay Wildlife Sanctuary, our mission is to conserve and protect the natural beauty and biodiversity of our environment while providing educational and recreational opportunities for all visitors.

We are dedicated to:

  • Preserving the park's delicate ecosystem through sustainable management practices.
  • Offering educational programs and guided tours to promote environmental awareness and appreciation.
  • Engaging with local communities to foster stewardship and support conservation initiatives.
  • Continuously improving and expanding our facilities and services to enhance visitor experiences.

By fulfilling our mission, we strive to ensure that Amuay Wildlife Sanctuary remains a thriving sanctuary for future generations to enjoy and cherish.
